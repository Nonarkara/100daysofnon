Open Questions

what he asked, and never went back to close

He is a man who ends on the question, never the answer. The workshop read all ninety-nine essays and kept the ones he asked of himself and left open. They are printed here word for word, each with a dashed line where the answer would go. Some of the hundred days exist to close a few of these. Most, he suspects, never will.
