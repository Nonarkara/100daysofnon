- Symptoms: shortness of breath, chest pain, collapse — often mistaken for a heart attack
- **Prevention**: blood thinners (anticoagulants) are standard post-surgical protocol
- If prophylaxis was **not given** or was **insufficient**, this constitutes **medical negligence**

**Timeline:**
