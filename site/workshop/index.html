The corrections — where the verdict reversed

  • “near Siam Paragon” → the Siam InterContinental, gone since 2002
  • “October” → the third of November; the photograph corrected him
  • “the worst years of my life” → the best ones, seen late
  • “my parents never saw what I was” → his mother was right; he never studied
  • “I know why I left Sydney” → he does not; the reason is still on its way

A life is mostly memory; the record only catches the edges. Three days are filed. Ninety-seven are still only remembered — which is exactly why the verdict keeps reversing, on a delay he cannot feel.
