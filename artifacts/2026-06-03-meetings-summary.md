# Chula Smart Campus Meeting 2 (2026-06-03)

**Source**: `resources/ai summaries/Chula_SmartCampus_Meeting_Transcript_EN.docx`
**Event**: Follow-up meeting regarding Chula Smart Campus platform. Non presenting the prototype and deep-diving into specific use cases with the maintenance, energy, GIS, and administration teams.

## Key Themes & Use Cases Discussed
- **Energy & Occupancy**: Linking room booking data with IoT sensors to automatically shut off AC in empty rooms. Non noted this solved the "last-mile problem" of behavioral change.
- **Contracts on the Map**: Integrating tenant and contract data into the 3D map. Technical hurdle: inconsistent building nomenclature across Chula's databases.
- **Safety & CCTV**: Rather than naive live-feeds, Non proposes AI detection specifically for student safety incidents near rooftops (high risk of jumping/falling) with zero data retention.
- **Infrastructure & Maintenance**: Using pressure sensors for water mains and crowdsourced photo reporting.
- **Deployment & Architecture**: Moving from Non's localhost home server (handling 70,000+ entries) to a cloud/IT server.
- **The Core Philosophy**: Non states he is an architect, not a programmer. He wants data to make spaces more *humane*, not just more efficient. 
- **Next Steps**: Non to draft a Research Proposal (standalone Dashboard vs. 12-month managed service).

# EAD Horizon AI Meeting (2026-06-03)

**Source**: `resources/ai summaries/EAD_Horizon_AI_Transcript_Cleaned_EN.docx`
**Event**: Meeting with EAD to discuss their "Horizon AI" educational/assessment platform.

## Key Themes
- **Purpose-Driven Assessment**: Non pushed them to shift from certification-driven to purpose-driven learning. Connecting users directly to employers based on *tasks*, not roles.
- **Sovereign AI & Public Projects**: Non expressed skepticism about a 1.6 billion THB national AI initiative that bypassed him, noting the danger of public money just passing through as kickbacks to ChatGPT wrappers.
- **National TV Platform**: Non discussed his 0-baht home-server prototype for national broadcasting, accessibility (Tesseract subtitles), and the political hurdles of doing it at a national scale without a 100-million-baht consultant fee.
- **Next Steps**: Draft a 200k-300k THB proposal for advisory/build engagement over 6 months.
