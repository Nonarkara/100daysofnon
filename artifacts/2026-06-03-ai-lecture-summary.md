# AI Tools for Media & Non-Media Lecture (Student Summary)

**Source**: `resources/สรุปแบบย่อ วิชา AI ดร.นนท์ 28 ธ.ค.69.pdf` (Note: Dated 28 May 2026 in the doc header)
**Event**: DEPA Workshop on AI Tools, 27-28 May 2026 at Baiyoke Sky Hotel.

## Overview
Dr. Non taught by demonstrating **12 real-world projects** currently deployed, rather than starting with abstract definitions.

## The 12 Projects Showcased
1. **National TV Platform (NBTC pilot)**: Streaming 21 channels over 4G/5G for remote areas. Built with an "invert image" feature for the visually impaired.
2. **World Cup Switcher**: Automated code to switch broadcast channels immediately based on schedules.
3. **Personal Finance AI**: AI as a personal financial advisor analyzing stocks, money markets, and gold.
4. **Thailand Smart City Dashboard**: Aggregating 4 research papers into an investment map for foreign investors (covers economy, climate, BOI privileges).
5. **Global Livable City Ranking**: Showing Bangkok at #1 for tourist arrivals, analyzing cost of living vs. food prices.
6. **Chula Space Management**: A digital twin of Chula linking NASA satellite data for traffic analysis and simulating building energy use.
7. **Phuket Smart Bus**: Demand-supply AI analysis to route buses from the airport to maximize profit and optimize paths.
8. **Smart City Chonburi**: AI mapping population density and alerting officials to disease outbreaks or mangrove encroachment.
9. **Writing a Book in 1 Hour**: Setting up a team of AI agents (researcher, reviewer, editor) to write about social inequality and budgeting.
10. **Massage Shop Directory**: AI scraping TikTok/Google/Telegram images to automatically build a directory.
11. **Tao Te Ching Reading Site**: AI summarizing 10 books, teaching Chinese, and building the final website automatically.
12. **Focus System**: Helping users stay focused on the screen.

## Key Takeaways Taught
- **"Command AI like managing a team of 30 people."** Assign specific roles (researcher, marketer, UI tester).
- **The UN Advice**: (1) Just use it, don't be afraid. (2) Build Trust (understand data privacy). (3) Use Incentives (humans need motivation, not just orders).
- **Final Quote**: "Attitude cannot be taught — you have to try it yourself first, just like parents have to get out of bed before they can teach their kids to."
