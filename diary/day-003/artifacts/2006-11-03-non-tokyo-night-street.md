In Day 3 Message 2, Non placed the trip in **October 2006**. The photo's date stamp shows **November 3, 2006**. Non flagged this himself in Msg 5 — he must have got the date wrong from memory. So the trip and the Chiba-hotel father reunion happened in **early November 2006**, not October.

This date correction also resolves the timing puzzle Non noticed in the same breath: Thai provincial governors normally take office on October 1. If the trip had been in October, his dad couldn't have travelled (would have already been in Buriram). Early November makes sense as an **off-season appointment** for the Buriram governorship — Non explicitly works this out in real time in Msg 5.
