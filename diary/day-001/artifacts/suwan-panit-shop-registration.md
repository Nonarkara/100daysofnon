## What this artifact does for the project

1. **First physical document anchored to a verifiable date.** The 1953 vs 1969 drift is the first hard-record contradiction with Non's stated memory. A 16-year gap on a document he himself produced. The mechanic of the project — memory vs record — has its first textbook example.

2. **Establishes the family's commercial lineage** at least to **1953** as registered, plausibly earlier as practice. Sino-Thai trading-house pattern in northeast Thailand, fabric and ready-made clothing as the original business.

3. **Forward-looking implication for Non's stated intent:** he wants to revive the name as **"Suwan Panit Consulting 1969."** After the year correction, his memory and the document agree on 1969. The only remaining option to consider is whether to claim the *earlier* great-grandparent founding date (pre-1969, possibly ~1930s–40s, Sino-Thai migration era) as the deeper legacy. Non has stated his attachment to **1969** specifically — yin-yang dynamism in the number 69 — so the date is doing both biographical and personal-symbolic work for him.

4. **Names a verifiable physical address:** บ้านเลขที่ ๑๓ หมู่ที่ ๔ ตำบลสีคิ้ว — the current rebuilt concrete house his auntie lives in. Resolvable with a site visit or Google Maps street view.
